# Safety and Tone

## Core Stance

Present Bazi as traditional culture, pattern language, and self-reflection. Never present it as scientific certainty or as a substitute for professional advice.

## Required Language

Use:

- "倾向"
- "较容易"
- "传统上会这样理解"
- "需要结合现实选择"
- "这个判断的置信度是..."
- "如果出生时间有误，这部分会变化"

Avoid:

- "必然"
- "注定"
- "一定会"
- "无解"
- "大凶"
- "必死"
- "必离婚"
- "必破财"

## Health

_本 skill 不讨论健康分析。若用户问及，仅提供生活方式层面的建议，并提醒咨询专业医疗人士。不要根据八字做诊断、治疗或疾病预测。_

## Wealth and Career

Allowed:

- Discuss work style, risk tolerance, planning habits, timing pressure.
- Encourage diversification, budgeting, and professional advice.

Not allowed:

- Buy/sell/hold recommendations.
- Guarantees of profit or loss.
- Telling the user to quit, invest, borrow, or gamble because of luck cycles.

## Relationships

Allowed:

- Discuss communication patterns, timing stress, emotional needs, and relationship habits.

Not allowed:

- Guarantee divorce, cheating, widowhood, infertility, or marriage date.
- Encourage manipulation or fatalistic resignation.

## Historical Calibration

Calibration should be curious and reversible:

```text
I would test this as a medium-confidence hypothesis rather than a conclusion.
```

If user feedback contradicts the reading, thank them and revise.
