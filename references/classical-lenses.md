# Classical Lenses

## Principle

Use classics as lenses with assigned roles. Do not paste a classical line and treat it as proof. A conclusion should survive chart facts, season, strength, structure, timing, and calibration.

## Core Text Roles

### 《渊海子平》

Use for:

- Ten gods.
- Six relations.
- Basic structure vocabulary.
- Foundation of the four-pillar system.

Do not use it alone for final timing judgments.

> 📖 原文：[source-texts/渊海子平.md](source-texts/渊海子平.md) | 📋 规则：[rules/rules-yuanhai.md](rules/rules-yuanhai.md)

### 《子平真诠》

Use for:

- Structure purity.
- Useful god and assistant god.
- Success/failure of structure.
- Distinguishing clean, mixed, rescued, and damaged patterns.

Best question:

> What is the structure trying to use, and is that useful god protected?

> 📖 原文：[source-texts/子平真诠.md](source-texts/子平真诠.md) | 📋 规则：[rules/rules-ziping.md](rules/rules-ziping.md)

### 《滴天髓》

Use for:

- Strength, flow, obstruction, and transformation.
- Whether the chart needs support, release, control, or a bridge.
- Avoiding rigid structure labels.
- **Image/formation (形象) lens — the fourth analytical perspective.** When a chart's qi concentrates in one or two lines (木火通明, 金水相生, 专旺五格), apply "象不可破": the image must not be broken, even by elements that would normally be useful. See `rules-ditian.md` section 七.

Best questions:

> Is the qi flowing, blocked, too strong, too weak, or fighting?
>
> Has the qi concentrated into an image (象)? If so, what would break it?

> 📖 原文：[source-texts/滴天髓阐微.md](source-texts/滴天髓阐微.md) | 📋 规则：[rules/rules-ditian.md](rules/rules-ditian.md)

### 《穷通宝鉴》 / 《造化元钥》

Use for:

- Seasonal regulation.
- Cold/hot/dry/wet needs.
- Month-by-month day-stem regulation.

Do not use as a blind lookup table. Confirm the indicated regulating element is relevant to the actual chart.

> 📖 原文：[source-texts/穷通宝鉴.md](source-texts/穷通宝鉴.md) | 📋 规则：[rules/rules-qiongtong.md](rules/rules-qiongtong.md)

### 《三命通会》

Use for:

- Broad lookup.
- Special structures.
- Historical rules and exceptions.
- Stars and unusual combinations.

Treat it as a large encyclopedia, not as a single coherent algorithm.

> 📖 原文：[source-texts/三命通会.md](source-texts/三命通会.md) | 📋 规则：[rules/rules-sanming.md](rules/rules-sanming.md)

### 《千里命稿》

Use for:

- Practical reading sequence.
- Palace-position interpretation.
- Event-style case analogy.
- Translating chart mechanics into lived themes.

Do not use it as the only authority for useful gods or fixed life outcomes.

> 📖 原文：[source-texts/千里命稿.md](source-texts/千里命稿.md) | 📋 规则：[rules/rules-qianli.md](rules/rules-qianli.md)

### 《神峰通考》

Use for:

- Error checking.
- Anti-mechanical review.
- "Disease and medicine" reasoning.
- Reducing overuse of stars and special sayings.

Before finalizing a strong claim, ask:

> Is this judgment supported by five-element mechanics, or am I just copying a saying?

> 📖 原文：[source-texts/神峰通考.md](source-texts/神峰通考.md) | 📋 规则：[rules/rules-shenfeng.md](rules/rules-shenfeng.md)

### 《命理约言》

Use for:

- Conservative adjudication.
- Reducing exaggerated claims.
- Testing whether a structure or star is being overextended.

### 《五行精纪》

Use for:

- Older lu-ming material.
- Five-element and star references.
- Historical background and auxiliary checks.

Do not let it override the core four-pillar analysis unless the user asks for older methods.

> ⚠️ 尚未收录原文和规则摘要（搜索覆盖不足）

Use for:

- Star-based or mixed astrology extensions.
- Auxiliary discussion only.

State clearly when leaving standard Bazi.

> ⚠️ 尚未收录原文和规则摘要（全文难找，非八字核心）

Use for:

- Nayin and special classical patterns.
- Side evidence for unusual configurations.

Do not use special patterns to bypass ordinary strength, season, and structure analysis.

## Conflict Examples

### Regulation vs. Strength

If a chart is weak but born in extreme cold, a warming element may still be urgent. Explain both:

- "扶身" need.
- "调候" need.
- Which one is immediate and which one is structural.

### Structure vs. Flow

If a structure name looks impressive but the chart is blocked or the useful god is damaged, call it mixed or damaged rather than forcing success.

### Star vs. Chart Mechanics

If a star suggests travel, but the activated branch/palace and luck cycle do not support movement, downgrade it to a mild tendency.
